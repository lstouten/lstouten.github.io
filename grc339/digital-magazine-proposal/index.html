Introduction

Frosted

Frosted is a digital magazine about the art of cake decorating. It can be challenging to find good looking cake design inspiration that isn't for a kid's birthday party or a wedding, so Frosted aims to showcase creativity via the visual medium of frosting. Frosted's aesthetic is best described as colorful retro cookbooks meets clean, geometric design. It is heavily influenced by the incredible artist Alana Jones-Mann, who creates gorgeous geometric pointillism cakes. Her cakes can be viewed at alanajonesmann.com as well as in the inspiration board for Frosted.
